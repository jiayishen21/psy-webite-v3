Mission

A Career Lasts a Life

At PassionFruit Youth, we aim to foster a community that not only supports one-another’s ambitions, but also collectively builds on each other’s successes, breaking boundaries and reaching new heights. Through various high-impact community events, volunteer/career insight oppurtunities, self-growth workshops, and more, PFY has its sights on being the premier hub for cultivating and prospering dreams for youth.
